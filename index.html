Light Bulb

Light Bulb is a game where you have to turn on all the lights on the board.
You can turn on a light by clicking on it. When you click on a light, it and its adjacent lights will change their state.
The goal is to turn on all the lights on the board.
Lightbulb cannot illuminate each other.
Good luck!
NOTE: For editing the board/map, please click on the cell to change the state of the cell.
There are 5 states.
(plain, obstacle, obstacle with numbers from 0..4)
Click continuously until the cell becomes what you want.
